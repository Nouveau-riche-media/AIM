THE STORY ABOUT US

Augmented Information Mechanics is an Information Technology corporation committed to the use of technology for the social and economicdriven IT based products, services and tools. The corporation was established in March 2017 by David Iwarue and Emmanuel Okebugwu in reaction to the perceived gaps in the IT ecosystem with regards firstly understanding what the consumer requires and secondly innovatively providing solutions to these very clear needs.
